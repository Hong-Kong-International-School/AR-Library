Interactive Piano

Experience by Shu-Hong Wu, Class of 2024

Welcome to the interactive piano AR! In this experience, you will be able to see a life size representation of the piano as well as being able to actually play the notes!

Instructions:

  1. Tap on the image above
  2. Allow camera access
  3. Move the device so that you can place the piano on a vertical flat surface
  4. Tap on the keys to play the piano. Have fun!
