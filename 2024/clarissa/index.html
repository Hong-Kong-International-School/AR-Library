How Do I Get There?

Experience by Clarissa Chen, Class of 2024

Lodaing

This AR experience is to help people who do not know where certain places are in the school. There are instructions and also real life arrows to point you in certain places.

Instructions:

  1. Tap on the image above
  2. Allow camera access
  3. In order for the experience to appear when first clicking into it, position you phone in front of a vertical surface. When wanting to go into the directions to go to the main enterance position the camera on the floor to the right of the front of the High School gate.
