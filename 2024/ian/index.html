How to use a Water Fountain

Experience by Ian Cheong, Class of 2024

Instructions on how to use a water fountain and directions to other nearby fountains.

Instructions:

  1. Tap on the image above
  2. Allow camera access
  3. Point your phone at the water fountain next to the QR code
  4. Click on the black button to get directions to nearby water fountains
  5. Click on the green button to watch a demonstration on how to refill your bottle
