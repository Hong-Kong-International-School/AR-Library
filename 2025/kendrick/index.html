Bus Finder

Experience by Kendrick, Class of 2026

My AR experience allows the user to find their afterschool bus in an immersive method.

Instructions:

  1. Tap on the image above
  2. Allow camera access
  3. Have the physical image Kwoon Chung School bus in frame of the camera
  4. Navigate by pressing your bus time on screen
  5. Maintain the physical image in frame at first, then you can move around and look for your buses by looking for your bus number floating above the buses.
