AR CAD Viewer for VEX Robotics- Prototype

Experience by Ericsson, Class of 2025

View CAD models directly in Augmented Reality, superimposing it side by side with your physical robot or design.

Instructions:

  1. Tap on the image above
  2. Allow camera access
  3. Move your camera around the printed anchor cone until the anchor is found
  4. Watch the model project out of the anchor cone and start viewing!
